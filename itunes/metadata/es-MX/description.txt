Dino Eggs Saga, from the creator of Gold Miner and Shoot Dinosaur Eggs game comes the sequel of shooting eggs game.

Dinosaur queen kidnaps the dinosaur friends, she hides them behind the her eggs mess call the Puzzle, and you as the dino hero embark on the colorful, exciting and emotional adventure to rescue friends.
